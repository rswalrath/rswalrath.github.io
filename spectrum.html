Spectrum is Northeastern University's literary arts magazine. I served as its layout and design editor for the 2014-15 school year, using Adobe InDesign to create the calendar and biannual issue from work submitted by the Northeastern community. I also headed a layout committee, led art critiques at our weekly meetings and worked with other officers to select work for the publication.
